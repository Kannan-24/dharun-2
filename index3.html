Welcome to Ramaiah Aerospace Engineering!

Pioneering the future of aerospace innovation and research.

About Us

The Department of Aerospace Engineering at Ramaiah Institute of Technology is dedicated to fostering excellence in education, research, and innovation in the dynamic field of aerospace. We are committed to shaping future leaders and engineers who will contribute significantly to the global aerospace industry.

Our Legacy

With a rich history of academic rigor and groundbreaking contributions, our department has consistently produced top-tier professionals and researchers. We pride ourselves on a legacy of innovation that propels us forward in aerospace advancements.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to be a globally recognized center of excellence in aerospace education and research. Our mission is to provide quality education, foster research, and promote innovation to meet the challenges of the aerospace industry and society.

Advanced Facilities

Equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories and cutting-edge technology, our department offers an unparalleled learning environment. From wind tunnels to advanced simulation labs, students have access to the best resources.

Our Achievements

Our students and faculty consistently achieve recognition in national and international competitions, research publications, and industry collaborations. We celebrate a culture of continuous achievement and innovation.

Research Focus

Our research spans across various domains including aerodynamics, propulsion, aircraft structures, avionics, and space technology, contributing to advancements in both theoretical and applied aerospace sciences.

Explore Our Programs

Department of Aerospace Engineering, M. S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology

The Department of Aerospace Engineering at MSRIT was established in the year 2013. It offers undergraduate (B.E.) and postgraduate (M.Tech) programs in Aerospace Engineering. The department focuses on providing a strong foundation in aerospace fundamentals, coupled with practical exposure to cutting-edge technologies.

Undergraduate Program (B.E. in Aerospace Engineering)

The B.E. program is designed to equip students with comprehensive knowledge in areas such as aerodynamics, aircraft structures, propulsion, flight mechanics, and avionics. The curriculum integrates theoretical concepts with hands-on laboratory experience and project-based learning. Students gain expertise in aircraft design, manufacturing, maintenance, and operation.

Postgraduate Program (M.Tech in Aerospace Engineering)

The M.Tech program offers advanced specialization in various sub-fields of aerospace engineering. It emphasizes research and development, preparing students for careers in academia, R&D organizations, and advanced industry roles. Focus areas include advanced propulsion systems, computational fluid dynamics, composite materials, and guidance & control systems.

Key Features:

  • Well-equipped laboratories including Aerodynamics Lab, Structures Lab, Propulsion Lab, Aircraft Systems Lab, and Avionics Lab.
  • Experienced and highly qualified faculty with expertise in diverse aerospace domains.
  • Industry collaborations and guest lectures by leading aerospace professionals.
  • Opportunities for internships and real-world projects.
  • Focus on research and innovation, encouraging students to participate in national and international conferences.
  • Strong alumni network in prominent aerospace organizations like ISRO, HAL, DRDO, Boeing, Airbus, and various airlines.

About Ramaiah Aerospace and Research Forum (RARF)

The Ramaiah Aerospace and Research Forum (RARF) is a student-led initiative under the aegis of the Department of Aerospace Engineering. RARF serves as a vibrant platform for students to delve deeper into the fascinating world of aerospace, beyond the conventional classroom curriculum. We aim to foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and hands-on learning.

Our forum brings together enthusiastic students with a passion for flight, space, and everything in between. We organize workshops, technical talks, design challenges, and practical projects to enhance members' theoretical understanding and practical skills. RARF is where ideas take flight, theories become reality, and future aerospace pioneers are nurtured.

What We Do:

  • Conduct regular technical sessions and seminars by faculty and industry experts.
  • Organize hands-on workshops on topics like drone building, rocketry, CAD, and simulation software.
  • Facilitate participation in national and international aerospace competitions.
  • Promote research and development activities among students.
  • Provide a collaborative environment for peer learning and project execution.
  • Network with alumni and professionals in the aerospace sector.

RARF Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading student aerospace forum, recognized for fostering innovation, research, and a passion for aerospace, thereby contributing to the development of competent and creative aerospace professionals.

Mission

  • To provide a dynamic platform for students to explore, learn, and apply aerospace engineering principles through practical projects and activities.
  • To organize workshops, seminars, and guest lectures to enhance technical knowledge and soft skills of members.
  • To encourage participation in national and international aerospace challenges and competitions.
  • To promote interdisciplinary collaboration and foster a strong network among students, faculty, and industry experts.
  • To inspire the next generation of aerospace engineers and researchers to achieve excellence and drive innovation.

Aeromodelling Club

The Aeromodelling Club is where passion for flight takes concrete shape. Members design, build, and fly various types of model aircraft, from gliders to powered RC planes. We participate in national competitions and foster hands-on engineering skills.

UAV Club

The UAV Club focuses on the design, development, and application of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles. Members work on projects involving autonomous navigation, payload integration, and advanced drone technologies. We explore the future of aerial robotics.

Propulsion Club

The Propulsion Club delves into the fascinating world of aircraft and spacecraft engines. From jet propulsion to rocket science, members study various propulsion systems, conduct small-scale experiments, and stay updated with the latest advancements in powerplants.

Aerodynamics Research Group

Conducts cutting-edge research in high-speed aerodynamics, flow control, and novel wing designs for future aircraft and aerospace vehicles.

Avionics & Flight Control Systems Group

Focuses on the integration of electronic systems, navigation, communication, and robust flight control laws for safe and efficient aircraft operation.

Aircraft Maintenance & Reliability Group

Researches advanced maintenance techniques, predictive analytics for component failure, and reliability engineering to ensure aircraft safety and longevity.

Aircraft Accident Investigation Group

This group specializes in the principles and methodologies of aircraft accident investigation. Members study forensic analysis, human factors, systems failures, and regulatory compliance to understand and prevent aviation mishaps, drawing inspiration from agencies like NTSB and AAIB.

Advanced Computing Group

Explores cutting-edge computational methods and technologies applicable to aerospace challenges, including high-performance computing, big data analytics, and artificial intelligence for complex simulations and decision-making.

Quantum Computing Group

Focuses on the theoretical and practical applications of quantum computing in aerospace, including quantum-enhanced optimization for aircraft design, secure quantum communication for space missions, and quantum simulations of materials.
