About Ramaiah Aerospace Engineering

Welcome to the Department of Aerospace Engineering at Ramaiah Institute of Technology. We are committed to providing world-class education and fostering groundbreaking research in the dynamic fields of aviation and space technology. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with a strong foundation in aerospace principles, coupled with hands-on experience and exposure to cutting-edge advancements.

Our esteemed faculty comprises experts with vast academic and industrial experience, dedicated to nurturing innovation and critical thinking. We boast state-of-the-art laboratories, advanced simulation facilities, and strong industry collaborations, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for successful careers in aerospace and related sectors.

Our Academic Programs

Undergraduate (B.E.)

A comprehensive Bachelor of Engineering program focusing on fundamental and advanced aerospace concepts, preparing students for diverse roles in the industry.

  • Aerodynamics & Flight Mechanics
  • Aircraft Structures & Design
  • Propulsion Systems
  • Avionics & Control Systems

Postgraduate (M.Tech)

Specialized Master of Technology programs offering in-depth study and research opportunities in advanced aerospace domains.

  • Aerospace Structures
  • Propulsion Engineering
  • Space Technology
  • UAV Design

Research Programs (Ph.D.)

Doctoral programs fostering innovative research and development, contributing significantly to the advancement of aerospace science and technology.

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics
  • Advanced Materials
  • Hypersonic Aerodynamics
  • Satellite Systems

Our Cutting-Edge Research

Advanced Computing in Aerospace

Focuses on high-performance computing, AI/ML applications in aerospace design, optimization, and data analysis for complex systems.

Aerodynamics Research Group

Conducts cutting-edge research in high-speed aerodynamics, flow control, and novel wing designs for future aircraft and aerospace vehicles.

Avionics & Flight Control Systems Group

Focuses on the integration of electronic systems, navigation, communication, and robust flight control laws for safe and efficient aircraft operation.

Aircraft Maintenance & Reliability Group

Researches advanced maintenance techniques, predictive analytics for component failure, and reliability engineering to ensure aircraft safety and longevity.

Our Dynamic Clubs

The Ramaiah Aerospace and Research Forum is home to several vibrant student clubs, each dedicated to a specialized area of aerospace. These clubs provide practical experience, foster teamwork, and encourage innovation outside the classroom.

Aeromodelling Club

Focuses on designing, building, and flying remote-controlled aircraft. Members learn about aerodynamics, structures, and flight mechanics through hands-on projects and competitions.

UAV Development Team

Dedicated to the research, design, and fabrication of unmanned aerial vehicles for various applications, including surveillance, agriculture, and defense.

Propulsion Systems Group

Explores advanced propulsion concepts, rocket engine design, and experimental testing of various thrust-generating systems. Passion for power!
