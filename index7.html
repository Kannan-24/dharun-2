  • Keynote Speakers Announced: We are thrilled to announce our keynote speakers for RARF 2025: Dr. Evelyn Reed from NASA and Dr. Victor Zhou from SpaceX.
  • Sponsorship Opportunities: Companies interested in sponsoring RARF 2025 can find details on our website or contact us directly.
  • Travel Grant Applications: Limited travel grants are available for student presenters. Application deadline is August 15, 2025.
  • Poster Session Guidelines: Detailed guidelines for poster presentations at RARF 2025 are now available on the conference website.
  • Technical Program Committee Meeting: The next TPC meeting is scheduled for July 20, 2025, at 10:00 AM in Conference Room B.
  • Accommodation Information: Special rates available for conference attendees at partner hotels. Please check the 'Resources' section for booking links.
  • Visa Invitation Letters: International attendees requiring visa invitation letters can request them via the 'My Profile' section after registration.
  • Proceedings Publication: Accepted papers will be published in the RARF 2025 Conference Proceedings (ISBN forthcoming), indexed in major databases.
  • Feedback Survey: A pre-conference survey will be sent out next week to gather your input on preferred workshops.
